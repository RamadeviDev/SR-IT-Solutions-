Privacy Policy

Welcome to SR IT Solutions

Your privacy is crucial to us. This privacy policy is straightforward: we’re open about what we do and don’t do with any information you share with us.

Information We Don’t Collect

When you visit our site, we don’t collect any personal information about you unless you choose to give it to us. We don’t track you, and we don’t use cookies or any other tools to gather information about you or your browsing habits.

Information You Give Us

The only time we get your information is when you decide to give it to us. This happens when:

  • You Use Our Contact Forms: If you’re interested in our services and reach out through our forms, we’ll ask for some info. This includes your name, contact details, and whatever other information you decide to share about what you need from us. We only use this to respond to your query or provide the service you’ve asked about.
  • Newsletter Sign-Up: If you sign up for our newsletter, you’ll give us your name and email. We’ll only use this to send you the newsletters you asked for. Nothing more.

How We Use This Information

Any information you give us is just used to help you out. Whether that's responding to your questions, providing our services, or sending you our newsletter because you asked for it.

Keeping Your Data Safe

We take looking after your information seriously. We have measures in place to keep it safe and we only keep it as long as we need to for the purposes you’ve agreed to.

Your Rights

You’ve got rights over your information, like asking to see what info we have on you, getting us to correct anything that’s wrong or delete it if you want us to. If you want to talk about this, just get in touch.

Getting In Touch

If you have any questions about how we handle data, want to exercise your rights, or just want to know more, you can reach us by email at info@sritsolutions.uk.

What’s Next

We might need to change this policy as our business or laws change. If we do, we’ll let you know through our website.
